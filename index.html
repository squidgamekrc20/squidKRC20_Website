Squid Game Story

Squid Game, a South Korean survival drama series, took the world by storm in 2021. Created by Hwang Dong-hyuk, the show follows desperate individuals competing in deadly children's games for a chance to win a life-changing cash prize. Its unique blend of childhood nostalgia and brutal consequences, coupled with a stark critique of capitalism, resonated with audiences globally. The series became Netflix's most-watched show, amassing 1.65 billion viewing hours in its first 28 days. Its impact extended beyond streaming, influencing fashion, sparking global conversations, and even inspiring real-life (albeit less lethal) recreations of its games.
